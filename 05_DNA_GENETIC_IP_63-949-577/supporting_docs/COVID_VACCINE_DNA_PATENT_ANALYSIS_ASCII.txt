- **Samples:** 32 vials (16 unique lots) - Pfizer/BioNTech & Moderna
- **Method:** Qubit fluorometry + qPCR (two independent validation methods)

- **Total DNA per dose:**
  - Pfizer: 371-1,548 ng/dose
